Help To Keep Our Bees!

Bees are the most important species in the world. Bees pollinate 70 out of 100 world food crops. This feeds 90% of the world! That is extraordinary put into context! Without bees, humans would not survive. Bees are rapidly becoming extinct. 35 UK bees species are under threat of extinction, and all species face serious threats. Right now, they need us almost as much as we need them. What can humans do to save bees? You can become a beekeeper!
